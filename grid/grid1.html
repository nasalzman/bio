Downy Woodpecker (Dryobates pubescens)

Diet:

  • Insects
  • Berries
  • Acorns
  • Grains

Habitats:

  • Open woodlands
  • Parks and suburbs
  • Along streams

Identification features:

  • Males have red napes that stand out from their black and white body
  • Males and females are small and short-billed
  • Males and Females both drum on trees
