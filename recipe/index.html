Nathan's egg and tortilla recipe

Ingredients

  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • A few slices of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 blue corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Drizzle the olive oil on the pan and turn on the stove
  2. Scramble the eggs until solid
  3. Put the cheese in the tortillas and then put the eggs on top
  4. Keep the tortillas in the pan with the ingredients inside to warm them up
  5. Enjoy!
